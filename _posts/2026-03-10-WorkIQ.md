## 𝗧𝗵𝗲 𝗜𝗻𝘁𝗲𝗹𝗹𝗶𝗴𝗲𝗻𝗰𝗲 𝗦𝘁𝗮𝗰𝗸 𝗘𝗻𝗮𝗯𝗹𝗶𝗻𝗴 𝗠𝗶𝗰𝗿𝗼𝘀𝗼𝗳𝘁’𝘀 𝗡𝗲𝘅𝘁 𝗚𝗲𝗻 𝗔𝗜 𝗔𝗴𝗲𝗻𝘁𝘀

The announcement of Copilot Cowork is an important step toward more collaborative AI inside Microsoft 365. As an EA, I think, the more meaningful development is the architecture that enables these next generation agents.

### 𝗧𝗵𝗲 𝗔𝗿𝗰𝗵𝗶𝘁𝗲𝗰𝘁𝘂𝗿𝗲

What I see is Microsoft is moving from app specific AI to a platform level intelligence model powered by three layers:
  • WorkIQ
  • Work Fabric
  • Foundry IQ
Together, they shift Copilot from reacting to queries understanding organisational context, data relationships, and business processes. This makes agents more intelligent across workflows instead of staying confined to individual apps.

![WorkIQ](../assets/images/posts/2026-03-10/workiq.png)

### 𝗪𝗼𝗿𝗸𝗜𝗤

WorkIQ is the contextual & intelligent engine that gives agents meaningful organisational understanding. It provides:
  • A unified view of enterprise data across Microsoft 365, Dynamics, Power Platform, and connected systems
  • Insight into work patterns — collaboration behaviour, document relevance, timelines, decision flows
  • Contextual intelligence that helps agents interpret past meetings, project context, dependencies, and user habits
WorkIQ elevates agents from isolated assistants to context aware collaborators.

### 𝗪𝗼𝗿𝗸 𝗙𝗮𝗯𝗿𝗶𝗰

Work Fabric ensures AI has secure & governed access to organisational data. It brings together:
  • Microsoft 365 and Dynamics content
  • Third party and line of business data
  • Connectors, APIs, and multi model capabilities
It enforces permissions, compliance, data boundaries, and governance, which is critical for enterprise grade AI. This layer makes WorkIQ both operational and safe.

### 𝗙𝗼𝘂𝗻𝗱𝗿𝘆 𝗜𝗤

Foundry IQ enables organisations to build and run their own agents. It provides:
  • Tools for custom, domain specific agents
  • Secure integration with internal systems
  • Multi step automation and workflow orchestration
  • Control over model selection and agent behaviour
This gives enterprises the ability to shape AI around their own processes and constraints.

### 𝗪𝗵𝘆 𝗧𝗵𝗶𝘀 𝗠𝗮𝘁𝘁𝗲𝗿𝘀 𝗳𝗼𝗿 𝗘𝗻𝘁𝗲𝗿𝗽𝗿𝗶𝘀𝗲 𝗧𝗿𝗮𝗻𝘀𝗳𝗼𝗿𝗺𝗮𝘁𝗶𝗼𝗻

Together, WorkIQ, Work Fabric, and Foundry IQ establish AI as an architectural capability, not an add on. This stack enables:
  • Context aware, role relevant agents
  • Cross workflow reasoning and execution
  • Secure, governed data intelligence
  • Scalable and consistent automation
  • A unified intelligence layer across the organisation

## Enterprise Transformation

Enterprises must stop treating AI as isolated tools and start treating it as a core architectural foundation. Those who move early will lead the next era of intelligent workflows
